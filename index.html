If you've ever wondered what tranquillity feels like then you should really experience Tyddyn Tyfod

Set in a remote spot on the periphery of the Snowdonia National Park, with perfect panoramic views across the valley, Tyddyn Tyfod provides a luxury cottage retreat where you can hear nothing but birdsong, the stream flowing and the occasional tractor in a field across the way.
